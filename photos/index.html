Restoring
Memories

Where every repair becomes part of the story.

Elegant Rescues is a studio devoted to the restoration, maintenance and conservation of fine art and high-value objects. We treat each piece as irreplaceable — returning it to its rightful beauty while honouring the hand and history that made it.

In the spirit of kintsugi — the Japanese art of mending with gold — we believe a repair, done with care, need not be hidden. It can become a quiet testament to an object's endurance.

  • Fully insured handling
  • Conservation-grade materials
  • Discreet collection & delivery

Workshops

Classes & intimate events

We open the studio for hands-on classes and small gatherings where the principles of conservation are shared — from the chemistry of cleaning to the patient art of mending with gold.

Sessions are kept deliberately small, suited to collectors, makers and the simply curious who wish to understand how treasured things are kept alive.
